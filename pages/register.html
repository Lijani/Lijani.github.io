How to become a part of the initiative

The initiative is aimed at empowering lower-income individuals in South Africa and aiding in managing waste responsibly.

An in-house developed registration system has been developed to allow waste pickers to register and become a part of the initiative. This system is deployed at partnering recycling companies, eliminating the need for waste pickers to be computer literate in order to join the initiative. All they need to do is go to the partnered recycling company closest to them with a valid form of identification.

After registering, their details are recorded and they receive their equipment. They can now receive payment at the Collection Centres when they bring in waste. Payment can be in the form of either cash, deposit into a bank account, or in the form of a gift card at a supermarket. The plan is to incorporate even more payment options in the future as the initiative grows and reaches new heights.

Recycling companies who want to become a part of the initiative can contact us to register. At the end of the day the initiative will benefit these companies as well since they will be able to collect waste that has already been sorted and is readily available - saving time by easing logistics.

Other individuals who want to become a part of the initiative can contact us to make donations or suggestions. Supporting the individuals who upcycle the waste is also a form of supporting the initiative - since it empowers these individuals and helps improve their quality of life.
