Where can you find us?

The initiative is located in South Africa. The team who developed the initiative is located in South Africa, Belgium, and the Netherlands respectively.

Our facilities are located in various geographic areas across South Africa. These facilities are what we call our Collection Centres. These Collection Centres comprise of a drop-off point for registered waste pickers, a waste processing system, a collection point for recycle centers and for registered users. Our unique smart bins can be found at these Collection Centres as well.

The Collection Centres were placed strategically in areas which is accessible by all parties - in essence the recycle companies, the registered waste pickers, and the communities who benefit from our Collection Centres.

Below is where you can find our current Collection Centres:

Gauteng

  • - Daveyton
  • - Atteridgeville
  • - Mamelodi

North-West

  • - Soweto

Gauteng is the most densely populated province in South Africa, which is why it was chosen as a staring point. The aim is to expand the Collection Centres all over the country. Additionally, the locations were chosen based on community density. The Collection Centres are placed close to highly populated communuities where a lot of waste is being generated and not being managed responsibly.
