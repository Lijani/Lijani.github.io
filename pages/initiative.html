About the Initiative

More information regarding our initiative
for addressing the waste problem in South Africa.

How it all started

It all started with a group of very different individuals who were given a problem. Facing this problem allowed these individuals to start working together and form a team - a team who could come up with a solution. Embracing the uniqueness of South Africa, the team developed an initiative to address the current waste problem in South Africa. The team believes that this solution will make a significant impact on various sectors in the country - including the lives of citizens and the environment.

Aim of the initiative

The aim of the initiative is to address the waste management issues in South Africa. Alongside this aim, the team aims to empower the lower-class citizens in the country, as well as empower the waste pickers. South Africa is a country with a very big class gap among the citizens, and with this initiative, the team hopes to shrink this gap and enrich the lives of the less-fortunate citizens.
