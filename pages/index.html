Heartland Equestrian Park

Our life. Our Story. The home of our horses.

Heartland Equestrian Park, founded in 2019, is a top-class equestrian facility situated in the North West Province. Looking out on the majestic Magalies mountain range, the whole feeling at Heartland is incomparable.

The people behind Heartland has a great love for horses and aspire to pass it onto every person walking into the facility. Every detail has been thought through and implemented with love, from planting the grass to putting the outside lights in.
