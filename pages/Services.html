The Services We Offer

Heart Land Equestrian Park offers top class livery and riding facilities and services, which include:

▪   Spacious, barn-style stables
▪   Irrigated paddocks with electric fencing and shading
▪   60x100m Klopf Fiber Arena
▪   60x80m Ebb&Flow Arena
▪   50x70m Klopf Fiber Arena
▪   25m Lunge arena
▪   Horse walker
▪   Solarium Facilities
▪   Show standard jumping equipment
▪   Full-time stable manager who lives on the property
▪   Kind, experienced grooms
▪   Qualified instructors - for all ages, levels and disciplines
▪   Feed warehouse
▪   Secure tackroom with an alarm system

The happiness of the horses in our care is our top priority. We feed only the best and cater for each horse and owner's individual needs.

Feel free to contact us for an appointment!
