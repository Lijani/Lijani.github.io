Data Visualization

Part of the initiative is to collect data about the types of waste being generated in the country, and where it is being generated. This data is to be used to make predictions of where the Collection Centres would be most ideal.

Below is a map containing our current Collection Centres and potential locations for new centres. 


                    

                    

                        The Collection Centres have been placed strategically close to high density areas and within reasonable driving distance from
                        existing recycling centres. This is to enable registered waste pickers to no longer need to travel extensive distances to get paid
                        for the waste they have collected; as well as enable the materials made available to lower income citizens accessible.
